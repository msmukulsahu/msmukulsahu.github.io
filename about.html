WE ARE SOLUTION

FOR STUDENTS

Mekanika is official society of the Department of Mechanical Engineeirng, IIT kharagpur. The society, rechristened in the year 2005 has been aggressivley involved in activities that would directly benefit the students of the department and serve as a platform for greater interaction between faculty and the students and among the student community themselves.

WHAT WE DO

Apart from organizing traditional events like Freshers’ Introduction and Final Year Farewell, Mekanika distinguishes itself by conducting several innovative and useful events which received huge particpation and appreciation by the student community in the preceding years. The Society conducts Training Seminar to help students explore internship opportunities and the correct ways to approach and acheive them. To help students gain a desired comfort level in softwares relevant to mechanical enginnering, we are conducting a series of Software workshops. Its first session, Workshop on Matlab drew participation beyond capacity and received huge appreciation. To expose the students to the multiple facets of miraculuous mechanical enginnering we conduct educational trips to industries near by. In future , we would endeavour to organize some competitive events to help students gain further insight about the Department .
